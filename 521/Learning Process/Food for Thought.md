Malaria
- Uncomplicated
- Cyclical of
	- Chills, fever, sweats
	- every 2 days with
		- falciparum
		- vivax
		- ovale
	- every 3 days with
		- malariae (chronic)
- 7-30 days post initial exposure
- Severe
	- cerebral malaria
		- abnormal behaviour
		- seizures
		- coma
		- altered mental status
	- Jaundice
	- Shock
	- Edema/ Resp failure
