- RNA non-enveloped
- Most common cause of common cold
- 100+ serotypes
- Mode of transmission
	- Respiratory droplets/aerosols
		- Coughing, sneezing
	- Indirect
		- Hands of infected people; fomites
- Replicates better in colder body regions
- Sneezing, nasal discharge, sore throat, cough, HA
