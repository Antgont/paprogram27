## Clinical 
- Clonorchis & Opisthorciasis
	- Asymptomatic +/- eosinophilia 
	- Acute: fever, abdominal pain, hepatomegaly, urticarial 
	- Chronic: localized obstruction of biliary tract (inc risk cancer)
