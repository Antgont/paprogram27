## MOA
- Inhibits fungal cytochrome P-450 reducing sterol synthesis and increasing toxic sterol accumulation
## AE
- Nausea, liver dysfunction

Other: drug-drug interaction as both inhibit CYP-450

- [[Fluconazole]] (diflucan)
- [[Itraconazole]] (Sporanox)
- Pro tip: flucanzole is first line for most systemic infections due to Candida, [[Itraconazole]] reserved form unusall or resistant fungus
